Expand description

Semantically fixed structural environments in scientific documents, to collect as add-on to the AMS markup

Note we are explicitly ignoring some of the very high-frequency environments, as they are not rich on textual content. Namely: references, appendix, pacs, subject; Which are rich in metadata and semi-structured content (figures, tables).
